Creating Lasting Change Through Collective Action

Queens of Change Foundation is a movement driven by compassion, courage, and the belief that every girl deserves the freedom to live with confidence and dignity.

We work closely with communities to create awareness around menstrual health, encourage open conversations, and support young women.

At Queens of Change Foundation, we are building more than awareness — we are building confidence, opportunity, and a stronger future for generations of women and girls.

Our Focus Areas

We work across multiple interconnected areas to create comprehensive and lasting change in communities.

Women Empowerment

Providing skills training, leadership development, and economic opportunities to help women become self-reliant.

Health Awareness

Breaking stigma through education, awareness programs, and healthcare initiatives in underserved communities.

Education

Supporting access to quality education, scholarships, and learning resources for girls and young women.

Social Impact

Building sustainable community development programs that foster inclusion and long-term growth.
